Open in explorer datafiles folder and add 360 images.
To add 360 photos you need to cut them into 2 halves. 
left half 180 degrees and right half 180 degrees. 
You can use gimp or any other program. 

High quality - scale the image to a resolution of 8192x4096pix and cut it into 2 parts. 
Sign the halves as 0HL.jpg and 0HP.jpg 

Normal quality - scale the photo to the size of 4096x2048pix, 
cut it in half and save as 0L.jpg and 0P.jpg 

add subsequent photos by increasing the number 1HP.jpg and 1HL.jpg. 

Put the files into the datafiles folder.
